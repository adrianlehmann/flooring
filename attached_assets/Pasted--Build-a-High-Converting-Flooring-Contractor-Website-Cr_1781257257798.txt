# Build a High-Converting Flooring Contractor Website

Create a modern, professional, trust-focused website for a flooring installation company called:

**3 Monkeys Floor Installation**
South Jordan, Utah

## Project Goal

Design a premium local contractor website that generates phone calls and quote requests from homeowners, property managers, real estate professionals, and small businesses looking for flooring installation services.

The website should immediately communicate:

* Professional craftsmanship
* Fair and transparent pricing
* Reliability and punctuality
* Outstanding customer communication
* Owner-operated service
* High-quality flooring installations
* Local Utah expertise

The overall style should feel similar to premium home service brands rather than cheap contractor websites.
